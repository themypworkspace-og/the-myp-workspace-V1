## Our Commitment to Student Privacy
The MYP WorkspaceŌäó is built with a **Privacy-First** mindset. 
* **No Backend Tracking:** This application does not use a database to collect student answers or personal info.
* **Local Processing:** All data (Equation editor inputs, session progress, and pasted screenshots) stays within your browser's Local Storage or is exported as a `.mws` file on your own computer.
