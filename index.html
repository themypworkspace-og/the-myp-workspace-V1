Detailed Instructions

1. Find a Paper: Search for an MYP Past Paper online and copy its URL. Paste it into the blue box above.

2. Set the Pace: Adjust the timer to match the official exam duration (usually 1h 30m or 2h).

3. Use the Tools: Open the Equation Editor for math, Polypad for geometry, and Desmos for calculations.

4. Transfer Work: Use the 📎 Upload Image button in any answer box to insert images directly. Or snip with Win+Shift+S (Windows) / Cmd+Shift+4 (Mac) and Paste (Ctrl+V / Cmd+V).

5. Save & Resume: Click "Save Session" to download a .mws file — open it in any text editor to read your answers offline, or upload it here to continue editing. Click "Save as PDF" for a printable copy.

Our Story

My name is Yogen Jayesh Patel. Transitioning from IGCSE to the MYP e-Assessment in Grade 10 was a massive shift. I realized that while we have access to "Formative," we could only use it for teacher-assigned tasks.

When I wanted to practice past papers independently, I found myself stuck between a PDF and a Google Doc, which feels nothing like the real exam software. I created The MYP Workspace™ to provide every student with a realistic, high-fidelity practice environment. This platform is built for students, by a student, to ensure no one enters their finals feeling unfamiliar with the digital tools.
