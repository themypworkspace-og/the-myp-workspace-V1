How to Use

  1. Find a Paper: Paste a PDF URL, or upload one from your computer.
  2. Set the Timer: Match the official exam duration (usually 1h 30m or 2h).
  3. Use the Tools: Equation Editor, Polypad, Drawing Tool, and Desmos are all built in.
  4. Add Images: Use 📎 Upload Image or paste with Ctrl+V / Cmd+V in any answer box.
  5. Save & Resume: Click "Save Session" for a .mws file. Auto-save also keeps a recovery copy in your browser.
💾

Auto-Save & Recovery

The workspace now auto-saves your progress to your browser every 30 seconds and whenever you type. If you accidentally close the tab or reload, click "Restore Session" on this screen to pick up exactly where you left off.

For permanent saves, still use 💾 Save Session to download a .mws file — this works across devices and browsers.

Our Story & Support

Built by Yogen Jayesh Patel . Transitioning from IGCSE to the MYP e-Assessment in Grade 10 was a massive shift. I realized that while we have access to "Formative," we could only use it for teacher-assigned tasks. When I wanted to practice past papers independently, I found myself stuck between a PDF and a Google Doc, which feels nothing like the real exam software. I created The MYP Workspace™ to provide every student with a realistic, high-fidelity practice environment. This platform is built for students, by a student, to ensure no one enters their finals feeling unfamiliar with the digital tools.

This is a live project. If you find a bug or have a suggestion to make the experience more realistic, please reach out.
